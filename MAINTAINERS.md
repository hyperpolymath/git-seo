### Lead Maintainer

### Maintainer

- Issue triage
- Community support
- Documentation review

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors who have demonstrated:

1. Consistent quality contributions
2. Understanding of project goals and RSR principles
3. Positive community interaction
4. Technical competence in Julia

May be invited to become maintainers.
